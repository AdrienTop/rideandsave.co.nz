Fuel consumption data

Official figures for
New Zealand's best-selling vehicles

Official NZ fuel consumption from NZTA full fleet data, with real-world estimates based on mandatory on-board monitoring of 600,000 vehicles — published by the European Commission.

Where this data comes from

Vehicle rankings and fuel consumption figures come from the NZTA Motor Vehicle Register.
Each model's figure is the average fuel consumption across all NZ registrations of that model across all years. Models are ranked by total NZ registration count.

How we estimate real-world consumption

Official figures come from standardised lab conditions — no hills, no AC, no traffic. The European Commission published real-world data from mandatory on-board fuel consumption monitors (OBFCM) fitted to 600,000 vehicles. The gap between lab and road is +23.7% for petrol and +18.1% for diesel.   → EU Commission OBFCM Report (March 2024)
